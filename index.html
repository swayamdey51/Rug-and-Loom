The Process

Our Craftsmanship

True luxury cannot be rushed. From the hand-spun wool of Ladakh to the intricate knots tied in Chamoli, every Rug & Loom piece is a testament to the art of slow creation.

Our foundation is built upon an unwavering commitment to the people behind the looms. By partnering directly with a dedicated community of about 1,000 artisans, we honor our ethical obligation to ensure their continued work, securing both their prosperity and the survival of centuries-old weaving heritage.

The Founders

Our Story

From the high-altitude passes of Spiti to the rhythmic clatter of the loom, our journey began not as saviors, but as students of a masterful art form.

We aren't here for charity. We are here to archive genius. Discover how two high school friends turned a vision incubated at IIM Bangalore into a mission to document, protect, and elevate the endangered legacy of Indian hand-weaving.
